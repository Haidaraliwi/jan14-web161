Social Media Optimization

SMO

SMO (Social media optimization) SMO is social media optimization is the use of social media networks to manage and grow on organization message and online presence. It can be used to increase awareness of new products, and services, connect with customers. SMO focuses only on driving traffic via various social media platforms and involves finding out what content performs well for your company on social platforms. also, SMO is the next wave of online marketing and is the perfect marriage between SEO and social media. Furthermore SMO is the technique designed to promote your product, service through various channels like youtube ,networking sites, twitter and LinkedIn. As a user SMO choose right platforms to focus on and these should be one of your audience. In this it is to find what social media platforms your audience use. The next is to perform competitive analysis. You want to figure out how you stack up against the competition. You can also perform content gap analysis on your social media, and once you understand your audience its time to build a strategy to help you reach the goals. The SMO is a very important tool, it ensures a strong web presence for your business.It introduces people to the business and also helps in branding and improving brand visibility. It provides the opportunity to reach out to target audience .It allows you to position the brand to your customer through mobile ,video and audio. Although social media is useful for branding, it is increasingly being used for lead generation. Social media optimization is great source for traffic generation and allows you to reach customers worldwide and is cost effective . Driving traffic to your site is an organic process that takes time and one that never ends .For many companies twitter has become an indispensable part of their busniess plan .The goal is not to spend all time to get followers but quality and consistency will have long impact on how much traffic will be driven to your site .
